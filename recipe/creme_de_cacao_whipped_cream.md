### Ingredients

|       Amount | Ingredient     |
| -----------: | -------------- |
|       2 cups | Heavy Cream    |
|         2 oz | Crème de Cacao |
| 1 tablespoon | Powdered Sugar |

### Method:

1. Whisk just until the cream reaches stiff peaks.
