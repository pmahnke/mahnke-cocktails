### Ingredients

| Amount  | Ingredient               | Brand            |
| -------: | ---------------- | ------------------------- |
|   1.5 oz | Gold Rum         | Angostura 1919            |
|   0.5 oz | Sweet Vermouth   | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino |
|   0.5 oz | Grenadine        |
|   0.5 oz | Fresh Lime Juice |
| 4 dashes | Bitters          | Angostura Aromatic        |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Lime twist
- Special Prep: None
