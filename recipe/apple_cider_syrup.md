### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient  |
| ------: | ----------- |
|  4 cups | Apple Cider |
| 1 stick | Cinnamon    |
| 1 pinch | Salt        |

### Method

1. Bring 4 cups unfiltered apple juice (cider) to a boil with 1 cinnamon stick.
1. Carefully reduce to 25% (about 1 cup), 60–90 minutes.
1. Remove cinnamon, add a pinch of salt, strain.
1. Store chilled.
