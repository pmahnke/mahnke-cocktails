### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                    | Brand        |
| ------: | --------------------------------------------- | ------------ |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lightly scorched rosemary sprig
