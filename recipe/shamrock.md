### Ingredients

|     Amount | Ingredient       | Brand                            |
| ---------: | ---------------- | -------------------------------- |
|    1.5 oz. | Irish Whiskey    | Powers Gold Label                |
|    1.5 oz. | Blanc Vermouth   | Dolin Blanc Vermouth de Chambéry |
|    0.25 oz | Green Chartreuse | Green Chartreuse                 |
| 1 barspoon | Crème de Menthe  | Tempus Fugit                     |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Mint leaves
- Special Prep: None
