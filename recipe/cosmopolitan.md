### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient       | Brand           |
| ------: | ---------------- | --------------- |
|  1.5 oz | Vodka            | Valentine       |
| 0.75 oz | Triple Sec       | Luxardo Triplum |
|  0.5 oz | Cranberry Juice  |
|  0.5 oz | Fresh Lime Juice |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lime wheel
- Special Prep: Expressed orange oil
