### Ingredients.

|   Amount | Ingredient      | Brand                                      |
| -------: | --------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
|   1.5 oz | Irish Whiskey   | Redbreast 12 Year                          |
|   1.5 oz | Blanc Vermouth  | Dolin Blanc Vermouth de Chambéry           |
| 2 dashes | Absinthe        | St. George Verte                           |
| 3 dashes | Boker's Bitters | Fee Bros. Cardamom (Boker’s Style) Bitters |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Martini
- Garnish: Lemon peel
- Special Prep: Expressed lemon oil
