### Ingredients

| Amount  | Ingredient     | Brand                     |

| 0.25 oz | Cherry Heering | Cherry Heering            |
| 1 rinse | Absinthe       | St. George Verte          |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain

- Special Prep: None
