### Ingredients

|    Amount | Ingredient        | Brand                  |
| --------: | ----------------- | ---------------------- |
|    0.75oz | Falernum          | Portland Syrups        |
|      1 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice |
| 6 to 8 oz | Beer              | New Glarus Spotted Cow |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: High Ball
- Garnish: Lemon wheel
- Special Prep:
  1. Add falernum and lemon juice to a shaker and shake with ice
  1. Strain into a high ball glass
  1. Top with beer
