### Ingredients

|         Amount | Ingredient                                                | Brand        |
| -------------: | --------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
|           2 oz | Gin                                                       | The Botanist |
|         0.5 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice                                         |
|         0.5 oz | Fresh Lime Juice                                          |
|        0.75 oz | [Semi-rich Simple Syrup]({%link recipe/simple_syrup.md%}) |
| 6 to 10 leaves | Fresh Mint                                                |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Mint sprig
- Special Prep: None
