## The Original (Jerry Thomas)

### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient                                      | Brand               |
| -------: | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------- |
|     2 oz | Cognac                                          | Pierre Ferrand 1840 |
|   0.5 oz | [Orgeat Syrup]({%link recipe/orgeat_syrup.md%}) |
| 2 dashes | Boker's Style (Cardamom) Bitters                |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Expressed lemon oil
- Special Prep: None
