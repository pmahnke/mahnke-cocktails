### Ingredients

| Amount   | Ingredient     | Brand                      |
| -------: | -------------- | -------------------------- |
|     2 oz | Demerara Rum   | El Dorado 12 Year          |
|     1 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino  |
| 2 dashes | Bitters        | Angostura Aromatic Bitters |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: 
    - Low Ball
    - Large Ice Cube 
- Garnish: Lemon twist or cocktail cherry
- Special Prep: None
