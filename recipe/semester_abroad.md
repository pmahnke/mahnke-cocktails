### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                  |
| -----: | -------------- | ---------------------- |
|   2 oz | Rye            | Old Forester 100 Proof |
|   1 oz | Orange Curaçao | Marie Brizard          |
| 0.5 oz | Fernet Branca  | Fernet Branca          |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Orange zest
- Special Prep: Express orange oil over the cocktail.
