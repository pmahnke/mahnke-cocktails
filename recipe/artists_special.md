### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                            | Brand                               |
| ------: | ----------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
|    1 oz | Bourbon                                               | Old Grandad Bonded                  |
|    1 oz | Amontillado Sherry                                    | Lustau Amontillado Los Arcos Sherry |
| 0.75 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice                                     |
| 0.75 oz | [Groseille Syrup]({%link recipe/groseille_syrup.md%}) |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: None
