### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient       | Brand                 |
| -----: | ---------------- | --------------------- |
|   1 oz | Gin              | Martin Miller’s       |
|   1 oz | Fino Sherry      | Valdespino "Ynocente" |
| 0.5 oz | Cocchi Americano | Cocchi Americano      |
| 0.5 oz | Triple Sec       | Cointreau             |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Martini
- Garnish: Expressed orange oil and peel
- Special Prep: None
