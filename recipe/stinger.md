### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient      | Brand             |
| ------: | --------------- | ----------------- |
|    2 oz | Cognac          | Maison Rouge VSOP |
|    1 oz | Crème de Menthe | Tempus Fugit      |
| 1 spray | Absinthe        |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Open Pour
- Glassware: Low BallCrushed Ice
- Garnish: Sprig of mint
- Special Prep: Spray the absinthe over the top of the cocktail.
