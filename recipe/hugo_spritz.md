### Ingredients

|        Amount | Ingredient          | Brand       |
| ------------: | ------------------- | ----------- |
|        0.5 oz | Elderflower Liqueur | St. Germain |
| 4 to 6 leaves | Mint                |
|          3 oz | Prosecco            | Ruffino     |
|          1 oz | Soda Water          | Topo Chico  |

### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
  - Wine glass
  - On the rocks
- Garnish: Mint sprig and lime wheel
- Special Prep: Add the mint and elderflower liqueur to the glass and muddle. Allow to sit
  for a minute before adding the prosecco and soda water.
