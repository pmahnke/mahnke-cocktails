### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                     |
| ------: | -------------- | ------------------------- |
|  1.5 oz | Bourbon        | Old Grand-Dad             |
| 0.75 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino |
| 0.25 oz | Bénédictine    | Bénédictine D.O.M.        |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Cocktail Glass
- Garnish: Expressed lemon oil and peel
- Special Prep: None
