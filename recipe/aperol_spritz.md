### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient | Brand      |
| -----: | ---------- | ---------- |
|   2 oz | Aperol     | Aperol     |
|   3 oz | Prosecco   | Ruffino    |
|   1 oz | Soda Water | Topo Chico |

### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
  - Wine Glass
  - On the rocks
- Garnish: Orange wheel
- Special Prep: None
