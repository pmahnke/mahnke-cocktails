### Ingredients

|     Amount | Ingredient                                          | Brand                      |
| ---------: | --------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
|       1 oz | Irish Whiskey                                       | Redbreast 12 year          |
|     0.5 oz | Single Malt Scotch                                  | Laphroaig 10 year          |
|    0.75 oz | Sweet Vermouth                                      | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino  |
|    0.75 ox | Green Chartreuse                                    | Green Chartreuse           |
|     0.5 oz | Campari                                             | Campari                    |
|   2 dashes | Bitters                                             | Angostura Aromatic Bitters |
| 1 barspoon | [Cinnamon Syrup]({%link recipe/cinnamon_syrup.md%}) |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Lemon peel
- Special Prep: Expressed lemon oil
