### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient          | Brand    |
| ------: | ------------------- | -------- |
| 0.75 oz | Vodka               | Hangar 1 |
| 0.75 oz | Coffee Liqueur      | Kahlúa   |
| 0.75 oz | Irish Cream Liqueur | Baileys  |
| 0.75 oz | Heavy Cream         |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Chocolate sauce drizzle and grated chocolate garnish
- Special Prep: None
