### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient       | Brand                            |
| -----: | ---------------- | -------------------------------- |
| 1.5 oz | Gin              | Botanist                         |
|   1 oz | Vodka            | Union Horse Distilling Co. Rider |
| 0.5 oz | Cocchi Americano | Cocchi Americano                 |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Martini
- Garnish: Lemon Twist
- Special Prep: Lillet Blanc may be substituted for Cocchi Americano.
