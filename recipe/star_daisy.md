### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                                | Brand                                         |
| ------: | --------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| 0.75 oz | Navy Strength Gin                                         | Junipero                                      |
| 0.75 oz | Apple Brandy                                              | Laird’s Straight Apple Brandy Bottled in Bond |

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: None
