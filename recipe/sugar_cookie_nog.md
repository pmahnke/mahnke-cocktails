### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient    | Brand      |
| ------: | ------------- | ---------- |
|    2 oz | Irish Whiskey | Green Spot |
|    1 oz | Licor 43      | Licor 43   |
| 0.25 oz | Amaretto      | Luxardo    |
| 1 whole | Egg           |
|  0.5 oz | Half & Half   |
| 1 pinch | Salt          |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Edible glitter
- Special Prep: 
  - For best results in texture, dry shake (shake without ice) before chilling to agitate the egg.
  - Expressed lemon oil
