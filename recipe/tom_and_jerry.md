## Tom & Jerry Drink

### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient         | Brand               |
| ------: | ------------------ | ------------------- |
|    2 oz | Tom & Jerry Batter |
| 0.75 oz | Demerara Rum       | El Dorado 12 Year   |
| 0.75 oz | Cognac             | Pierre Ferrand 1840 |
|    3 oz | Hot Water          |

### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware: Tea Cup
- Garnish: Grated nutmeg
- Special Prep: Add the batter, rum and cognac to the cup and mix. Top with the hot water.

## Tom & Jerry Batter

### Ingredients

|         Amount | Ingredient       | Brand                      |
| -------------: | ---------------- | -------------------------- |
|        2 whole | eggs             |
|   0.5 teaspoon | Vanilla Extract  |
| 0.125 teaspoon | Ground Clove     |
| 0.125 teaspoon | Ground Allspice  |
| 0.125 teaspoon | Ground Cinnamon  |
|         0.5 oz | Jamaican Rum     | Appleton Estate V/X        |
|       0.5 cups | Granulated Sugar |
|        1 pinch | Salt             |
|         1 dash | Bitters          | Angostura Aromatic Bitters |

### Method

1. In a small bowl, combine spices and rum. Stir and set aside.
2. Separate eggs into two bowls.
3. Add sugar, vanilla extract, bitters, and spice mixture to the yolks. Stir to combine.
4. Add salt to egg whites and whip to stiff peaks.
5. Gently fold together egg whites with yolk mixture.
