### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient                                               | Brand                                 |
| -----: | -------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
|   2 oz | Jamacian Rum                                             | Hamilton Jamaican Black Pot Still Rum |
|   1 oz | Fresh Lime Juice                                         |
| 0.5 oz | [Rich Demerara Syrup]({%link recipe/demerara_syrup.md%}) |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lime wheel
- Special Prep: None
