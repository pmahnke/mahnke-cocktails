### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient              | Brand           |
| -----: | ----------------------- | --------------- |
|   2 oz | Barrel Strength Bourbon | Stagg Jr.       |
| 0.5 oz | Bénédictine             | Bénédictine DOM |
| 0.5 oz | Walnut Liqueur          | Nux Alpina      |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Low Ball, Large Ice cube
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: None
