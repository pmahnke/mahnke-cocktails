### Ingredients

| Amount  | Ingredient               | Brand                     |
| ---------: | -------------- | -------------------------------- |
|       2 oz | Gin            | Martin Miller's                  |

|     0.5 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Dolin Vermouth de Chambéry Rouge |
| 1 teaspoon | Bénédictine    | Bénédictine DOM                  |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Coupe

- Special Prep: Expressed lemon oil
