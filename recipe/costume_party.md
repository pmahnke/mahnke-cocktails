### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient           | Brand                         |
| ------: | -------------------- | ----------------------------- |
|    1 oz | Vodka                | Finlandia                     |
| 0.75 oz | White Crème De Cacao | Marie Brizard                 |
|  0.5 oz | Apricot Liqueur      | Giffard Abricot Du Roussillon |
|  0.5 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice    |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: Sugared Rim
