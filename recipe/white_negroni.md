### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient      | Brand        |
| ------: | --------------- | ------------ |
|  1.5 oz | Gin             | Plymouth     |
| 0.75 oz | Gentian liqueur | Suze         |
| 0.75 oz | Lillet Blanc    | Lillet Blanc |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: Grapefruit zest
- Special Prep: Express grapefruit oil on the drink.
