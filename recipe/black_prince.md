### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient     | Brand               |
| ------: | -------------- | ------------------- |
|    2 oz | Guatemalan Rum | Zacapa 23yr         |
| 0.75 oz | Punt e Mes     | Punt e Mes          |
|  0.5 oz | Amaro          | Averna              |
|  1 dash | Orange Bitters | Steve the Bartender |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: None
