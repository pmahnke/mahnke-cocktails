### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient  |
| -------: | ----------- |
| 1.5 cups | White Sugar |
|    1 cup | Hot Water   |

### Method

1. In a small sauce pan, combine sugar and water.
2. Stir over heat until sugar is completely dissolved.
