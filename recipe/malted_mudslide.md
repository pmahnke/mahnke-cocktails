This variation balances the rich sweetness of coffee and Irish cream liqueurs with the spirit's toasted grain and oak notes, resulting in a sophisticated drink with a lingering, savory finish.

### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient          | Brand           |
| -------: | ------------------- | --------------- |
| 3 scoops | Chocolate Ice Cream |
|   1.5 oz | Blended Scotch      | Monkey Shoulder |
|   1.5 oz | Coffee Liqueur      | Kahlúa          |
|     1 oz | Irish Cream Liqueur | Baileys         |
| 0.25 cup | Crushed Ice         |

### Notes

- Method: Blended
- Pour: Open Pour
- Glassware: High Ball
- Garnish: Grated chocolate
- Special Prep:
  - Blend cocktail with a stick blender in the shaking tin.
  - Pour cocktail into glass.
  - Top with [Baileys Infused Whipped Cream]({%link recipe/baileys_whipped_cream.md%}).
