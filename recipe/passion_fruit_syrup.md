### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient                |
| -------: | ------------------------- |
|    14 oz | Frozen Passion Fruit Pulp |
| 2.5 cups | White Sugar               |

### Method

1. In a medium sauce pan, combine passion fruit pulp and sugar.
2. Stir over heat until sugar is completely dissolved.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
