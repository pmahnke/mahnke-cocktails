### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient                                      | Brand      |
| -----: | ----------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
|   1 oz | [Grenadine]({%link recipe/grenadine_syrup.md%}) |
|   1 oz | Fresh Lime Juice                                |
|   5 oz | Sparkling Water                                 | Topo Chico |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - High Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Lime wheel and cocktail umbrella
- Special Prep: None
