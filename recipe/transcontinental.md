### Ingredients

|     Amount | Ingredient         | Brand               |
| ---------: | ------------------ | ------------------- |
|    1.75 oz | Gin                | Hayman's London Dry |
|    0.33 oz | Maraschino liqueur | Luxardo             |
| 1 barspoon | Yellow Chartreuse  | Yellow Chartreuse   |
|    0.25 oz | Crème de Violette  | Rothman & Winter    |
|     0.5 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice  |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lemon swath
- Special Prep: None
