### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient        | Brand                         |
| -------: | ----------------- | ----------------------------- |
|   .75 oz | London Dry gin    | Gin Mare                      |
|   .75 oz | Manzanilla sherry | La Goya or Tio Pepe or Lustau |
|   .75 oz | Lillet blanc      |
| 2 dashes | Orange bitters    |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lemon Peel
- Special Prep: Expressed lemon oil over cocktail
