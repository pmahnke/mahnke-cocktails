### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient       | Brand                  |
| ------: | ---------------- | ---------------------- |
|  0.5 oz | Vodka            | St. George All Purpose |
| 0.75 oz | Aperol           | Aperol                 |
| 0.75 oz | Cocchi Americano | Cocchi Americano       |
|    3 oz | Prosecco         |
|  0.5 oz | Soda Water       |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Lemon wheel garnish
- Special Prep: 
  1. Stir the cocktail till chilled
  1. Strain the cocktail over the large rock
  1. Top with prosecco and soda water
  1. Garnish with lemon wheel
