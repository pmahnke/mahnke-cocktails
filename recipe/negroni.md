### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                      |
| ------: | -------------- | -------------------------- |
| 1.25 oz | Gin            | The Botanist Islay Dry Gin |
| 0.75 oz | Campari        | Campari                    |
| 0.75 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino  |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: Flamed Orange Rind
