### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient | Brand |
| -----: | ---------- | ----- |
|     1 oz   |      Rye Whiskey      |   Rittenhouse    |
| 1 oz       |       Cynar     |   Cynar    |
| 1 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Carpano Antica Vermouth |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Lemon twist and a pinch of salt
- Special Prep: None
