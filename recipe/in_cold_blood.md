### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient     | Brand          |
| -----: | -------------- | -------------- |
|   1 oz | Amaro          | Cynar          |
|   1 oz | Sweet Vermouth | Carpano Antica |
|   1 oz | Rye            | Qhistlepig     |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Expressed lemon oil
- Special Prep: Lemon Twist
