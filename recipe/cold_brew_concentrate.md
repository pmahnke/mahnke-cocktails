### Ingredients

| Amount  | Ingredient            | 
| ------: | ---------------------- |
|  1 part | Coarsely Ground Coffee |
| 8 parts | Water                  |

### Method

1. Combine 1 part by weight coarsely ground coffee with 8 parts by weight cold water.
2. Stir to combine. Seal and store in fridge for 12-14 hours.
3. Strain off coffee grounds.
