### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                      | Brand          |
| ------: | ----------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
|    2 oz | Gin                                             | Beefeater      |
|  0.5 oz | Dry Curaçao                                     | Pierre Ferrand |
| 0.75 oz | [Orgeat Syrup]({%link recipe/orgeat_syrup.md%}) |
| 0.75 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice                               |
| 1 whole | Strawberry                                      |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Mint sprig
- Special Prep: 
  1. Add strawberry to the tin
  1. Muddle strawberry
  1. Build cocktail in other tin
  1. Add ice and shake
