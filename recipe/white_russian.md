### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient     | Brand     |
| -----: | -------------- | --------- |
|   1 oz | Vodka          | Finlandia |
|   1 oz | Coffee Liqueur | Kahlúa    |
|   1 oz | Heavy Cream    |           |
 

### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
    - Low Ball
    - On the Rocks
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: None
