### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient                                      | Brand                 |
| -----: | ----------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
|   2 oz | Rye                                             | Rittenhouse 100 Proof |
| 0.5 oz | Fresh Lemon Juice                               |
| 0.5 oz | Fresh Orange Juice                              |
| 0.5 oz | [Grenadine]({%link recipe/grenadine_syrup.md%}) |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Orange slice and cocktail cherry
- Special Prep: None
