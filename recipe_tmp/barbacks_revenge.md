### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient            | Brand            |
| -----: | --------------------- | ---------------- |
|   1 oz | Demerara Rum          | El Dorado 8 Year |
|   1 oz | Bénédictine           | Bénédictine DOM  |
| 0.5 oz | Amontillado Sherry    | Lustau Los Arcos |
|   1 oz | Cold Brew Concentrate |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Expressed lemon oil
- Special Prep: None
