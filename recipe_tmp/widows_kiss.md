## Original

### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient        | Brand                      |
| -------: | ----------------- | -------------------------- |
|     1 oz | Applejack         | Laird's Straight 86        |
|   0.5 oz | Bénédictine       | Bénédictine DOM            |
|   0.5 oz | Yellow Chartreuse | Yellow Chartreuse          |
| 2 dashes | Bitters           | Angostura Aromatic Bitters |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: None
