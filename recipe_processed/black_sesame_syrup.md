### Ingredients

### Method

1. Toast 2 cups black sesame seeds.
1. Simmer with 2 cups water for 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Strain
1. Mix in 1 <sup>1</sup>&frasl;<sub>2</sub> times sugar by weight plus a pinch of salt.
1. Cool and store in fridge.
