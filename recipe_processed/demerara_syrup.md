### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient     |
| -----: | -------------- |
| 2 cups | Demerara Sugar |
|  1 cup | Hot Water      |

### Method

1. In a small sauce pan, combine Demerara sugar and water.
2. Stir over heat until sugar is completely dissolved.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
