- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Single Strain
- Glassware:
  - High Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Inverted lime rind filled with the Crème de cassis for an “interactive” garnish
- Special Prep: Single strain into glass, top the drink with the ginger soda
