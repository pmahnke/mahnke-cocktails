### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Sugared rim & long lemon peel
- Special Prep: Heavily coat the outside of the glass with sugar. Wind the lemon peel around the inside of the glass.
