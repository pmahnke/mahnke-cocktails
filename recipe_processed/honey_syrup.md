### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient    |
| ------: | ------------- |
| 3 parts | Honey         |
| 1 parts | Boiling Water |

### Method

1. In a heat resistant bowl or glass, combine 3 parts honey and| 1 part hot water.
2. Stir until honey and water are completely integrated.
