### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Mint leaf
- Special Prep: Add mint leaves to the shaking tin and add other ingredients minus the champagne. Pour cocktail into glass and top it off with champagne.
