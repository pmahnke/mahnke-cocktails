### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Open Pour
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Crushed Ice
- Garnish: Grated nutmeg and mint sprig
- Special Prep: Open pour the cocktail into the glass. Top with additional crushed ice. Dash bitters on top.
