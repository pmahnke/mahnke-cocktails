### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: 
    - Julep Cup
    - Crushed Ice
- Garnish: Mint Sprig
- Special Prep: Add mint leaves directly to the shaking tin. Then build the cocktail. Top with additional crushed ice.
