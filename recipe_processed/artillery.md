### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                     |
| -------: | -------------- | ------------------------- |
|   1.5 oz / 37.5 ml | Gin            | Plymouth                  |
|   1.5 oz / 37.5 ml | Sweet Vermouth | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino |
| 2 dashes | Orange Bitters | Regan's No. 6             |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Cocktail cherry and or an orange twist
- Special Prep: None
