### Notes

- Method: Blended
- Pour: Open Pour
- Glassware:
  - Hurricane Glass
  - Crushed Ice
- Garnish: Strawberry or lime wedge
- Special Prep: Blend the first four ingredients without the ice to break down the strawberries. Then add the ice and blend to combine.
