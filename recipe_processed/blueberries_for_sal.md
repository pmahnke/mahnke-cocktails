### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - High Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Mint sprig
- Special Prep:
  - Press and slide the mint leaves in the glass.
  - Add the blueberries, simple syrup and lemon juice to a shaker and shake with ice.
  - Strain the cocktail into the glass.
  - Add ice
  - Top with the ginger beer.
