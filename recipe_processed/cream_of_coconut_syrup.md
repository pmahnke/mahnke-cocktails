### Ingredients

|    Amount | Ingredient                                |
| --------: | ----------------------------------------- |
| <span class="onex active">1.75 cups </span> <span class="onehalfx">2.625 cups </span> <span class="twox">3.5 cups </span> <span class="threex">5.25 cups </span>| White Sugar                               |
|   <span class="onex active">13.5 oz  / 338 ml</span> <span class="onehalfx">2<sup>1</sup>&frasl;<sub>4</sub> oz  / 506 ml</span> <span class="twox">27 oz  / 675 ml</span> <span class="threex">4<sup>1</sup>&frasl;<sub>2</sub> oz  / 1013 ml</span>| Unsweetened Full Fat Coconut Milk (1 Can) |
|   <span class="onex active">1 pinch </span> <span class="onehalfx">1.5 pinch </span> <span class="twox">2 pinch </span> <span class="threex">3 pinch </span>| Salt                                      |

### Method:

1. In a medium sauce pan, combine sugar, coconut milk, and salt.
2. Stir over heat until sugar is completely dissolved.
