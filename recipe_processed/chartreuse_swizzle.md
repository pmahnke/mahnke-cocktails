### Notes

- Method: Swizzled
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
  - High Ball
  - Crushed Ice
- Garnish: Grated Nutmeg and a Mint Sprig
- Special Prep: Swizzle in the glass, and top with additional crushed ice.
