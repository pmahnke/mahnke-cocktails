### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                      |
| ------: | -------------- | -------------------------- |
|  1.5 oz / 37.5 ml | Sweet Vermouth | Cocchi Vermouth di Torino  |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Triple Sec     | Cointreau                  |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Bitters        | Angostura Aromatic Bitters |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Expressed orange oil and peel
- Special Prep: None
