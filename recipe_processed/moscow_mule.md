### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
  - Copper Mug
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Large mint sprig
- Special Prep: Pour in the vodka, lime juice and bitters then top with the ginger beer.
