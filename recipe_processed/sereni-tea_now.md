### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                      | Brand |
| ------: | ----------------------------------------------- | ----- |
|    1 oz / 25 ml | Canned Pear (Puréed With Syrup)                 |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | [Grenadine]({%link recipe_processed/grenadine_syrup.md%}) |
|  0.5 oz / 12.5 ml | Fresh Lemon Juice                               |
|    2 oz / 50 ml | Chilled Hojicha (Roasted Green Tea)             |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Coupe
- Garnish: Lemon peel
- Special Prep: 
  - Take a can of pears and their syrup and blend them into a prrée.
  - Expressed lemon oil.
