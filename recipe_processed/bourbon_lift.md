### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: High Ball
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: Add cocktail to the glass. Top with soda water in two stages. The top will rise above the top of the glass, similar to a Ramos Gin Fizz.
