### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: Grape
- Special Prep: Muddle the grapes in the cheater tin. Put the other ingredients in the large tin, add ice, and shake.
