### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Single Strain
- Glassware: Cocktail Glass
- Garnish: Grated nutmeg
- Special Prep: For best results in texture, dry shake (shake without ice) before chilling to agitate either the egg white or aquafaba.
