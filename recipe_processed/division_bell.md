### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient         | Brand           |
| ------: | ------------------ | --------------- |
|    1 oz / 25 ml | Mezcal             | Del Maguey Vida |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Aperol             | Aperol          |
|  0.5 oz / 12.5 ml | Maraschino Liqueur | Luxardo         |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Fresh Lime Juice   |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Expressed grapefruit oil
