### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Sage leaf
- Special Prep: Dice the apple piece into 1/2 inch pieces, and drop them into the shaker tin. Muddle the apple piece in the shaker tin.
