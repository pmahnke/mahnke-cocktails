### Ingredients

|    Amount | Ingredient       |
| --------: | ---------------- |
|      6 oz | Water            |
| 12 sticks | Cinnamon         |
|  10 whole | Cloves           |
|  12 whole | Allspice Berries |

### Method

1. In a small sauce pan, combine water, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and allspice.
2. Bring to a boil.
3. Let rest until cool.
4. Strain.
