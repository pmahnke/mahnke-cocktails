### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Single Strain
- Glassware:
  - High Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Cucumber strip cut up, strawberries, and a sprig of mint
- Special Prep: Pour ginger ale and shaken Pimm's and lemon mixture into glass together.
