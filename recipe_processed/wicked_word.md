### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep:
  - Add the cognac, Aperol and lemon juice to a shaker and shake.
  - Pour the cocktail into a Nick & Nora glass.
  - Into a small snifter add the sambuca, and light it.
  - Pour the flaming sambuca on the cocktail and stir.
