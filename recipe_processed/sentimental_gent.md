### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient      | Brand           |
| -----: | --------------- | --------------- |
|   2 oz / 50 ml | Scotch, Blended | Monkey Shoulder |
| 0.5 oz / 12.5 ml | Bénédictine     | Bénédictine DOM |
| 0.5 oz / 12.5 ml | Walnut Liqueur  | Nux Alpina      |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware: 
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: None
