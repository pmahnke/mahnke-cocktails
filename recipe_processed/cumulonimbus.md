### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient                                          | Brand |
| ------: | --------------------------------------------------- | ----- |
|    2 Oz | Freshly Brewed Espresso                             |
| 0.75 Oz | [Cinnamon Syrup]({%link recipe_processed/cinnamon_syrup.md%}) |
| 3 Drops | Orange Blossom Water                                |
| <span class="onex active">0.75 oz / 19 ml</span> <span class="onehalfx">1.125 oz / 28 ml</span> <span class="twox">1.5 oz / 37.5 ml</span> <span class="threex">2.25 oz / 56.5 ml</span> | Egg White                                           |
|    1 Oz | Soda Water                                          |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: High Ball
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: 
  - Add half the soda water to the glass
  - Strain in the cocktail. 
  - Top with more soday water. 
  - Expressed orange oil. 
  - For best results in texture, dry shake (shake without ice) before chilling to agitate either the egg white.
