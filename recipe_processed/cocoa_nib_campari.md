### Ingredients

|       Amount | Ingredient |
| -----------: | ---------- |
| 1 tablespoon | Cocoa Nibs |
|        1 cup | Campari    |

Method:

### Method

1. Combine cocoa nibs with Campari.
2. Seal and shake.
3. Allow to infuse for 1-2 hours (I like a longer infusion).
4. Strain off cocoa nibs.
