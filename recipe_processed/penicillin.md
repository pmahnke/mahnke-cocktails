### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: Candied Ginger
- Special Prep: Muddle the ginger slices in the shaker tin until mushy. Then add the other ingredients, ice and shake.
