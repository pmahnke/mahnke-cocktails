### Ingredients

|          <span class="onex active">3 oz / 75 ml</span> <span class="onehalfx">4.5 oz / 113 ml</span> <span class="twox">6 oz / 150 ml</span> <span class="threex">9 oz / 225 ml</span> | Prosecco            | Ruffino     |

### Notes

- Method: Build in the Glass
- Pour: Add all ingredients directly to the glass
- Glassware:
- Garnish: Mint sprig and lime wheel Special
- Prep: Add the mint and elderflower liqueur to the glass and muddle. Allow to sit
  for a minute before adding the prosecco and soda water.
