### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Open Pour
- Glassware:
  - Brandy Snifter
  - Crushed Ice
- Garnish: 2 cocktail cherries (eyes), orange wedge (beak), 2 pineapple fronds (flippers), & cocktail umbrella for garnish (arranged to look like a penguin)
- Special Prep: None
