### Ingredients

|   Amount | Ingredient      |
| -------: | --------------- |
|    1 cup | 100% Guava Pulp |
|    1 cup | White Sugar     |
| 0.5 cups | Water           |

### Method

1. In a pot, combine ingredients over low heat until sugar dissolves.
2. Simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let cool.
