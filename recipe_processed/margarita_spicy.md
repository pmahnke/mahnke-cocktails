### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - On the Rocks
- Garnish: Lime wheel
- Special Prep: Muddle the jalapeño slices in the cheater tin before shaking. Tajin rim.
