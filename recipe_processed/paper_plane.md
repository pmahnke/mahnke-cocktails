### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient        | Brand              |
| ------: | ----------------- | ------------------ |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Bourbon           | Old Grandad Bonded |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Light Amaro       | Amaro Nonino       |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Aperol            | Aperol             |
| 0.75 oz / 19 ml | Fresh Lemon Juice |

### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: Nick & Nora
- Garnish: Small paper plane
- Special Prep: None
