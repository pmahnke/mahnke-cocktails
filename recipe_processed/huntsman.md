### Ingredients

| Amount | Ingredient     | Brand                 |
| -----: | -------------- | --------------------- |
|   2 oz | Rye            | Rittenhouse 100 Proof |
| 0.5 oz | Cherry Liqueur | Cherry Heering        |
| 0.5 oz | Bénédictine    | Bénédictine DOM       |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice Cube
- Garnish: None
- Special Prep: Expressed orange oil
