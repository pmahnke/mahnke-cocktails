### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
  - Low Ball
  - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: Orange Zest and Cocktail Cherry
- Special Prep: Smoke the low ball glass with wood smoke. Express the orange zest over the cocktail.
