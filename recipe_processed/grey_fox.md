### Notes

- Method: Shaken
- Pour: Double Strain
- Glassware: High Ball, On the Rocks
- Garnish: Lemon Zest, Sprig of Thyme or Lavender
- Special Prep: Pour in one ounce of tonic water, then pour in the cocktail. Top off with tonic water.
