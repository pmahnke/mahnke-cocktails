### Ingredients

|  Amount | Ingredient   | Brand        |

### Notes

- Method: Stirred
- Pour: Julep Strain
- Glassware:
    - Low Ball
    - Large Ice cube
- Garnish: Grapefruit zest
- Special Prep: Express grapefruit oil on the drink.
