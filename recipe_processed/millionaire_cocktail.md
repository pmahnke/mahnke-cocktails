  Recipe adapted from Harry Craddock's 1930 <i>The Savoy Cocktail Book</i>. Craddock also lists a "Millionaire Cocktail (No.2)" as follows: 
  <br>- 1 Dash Anisette
  <br>- The white of 1 Egg
  <br>- 1/3 Absinthe
  <br>- 2/3 Dry gin
  <br>- Shake well and strain into cocktail glass.
